Notakto

Notakto is a tic-tac-toe variant where the player who makes the last move loses. The game is played on a 3x3 board. The game is played by two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3x3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. The player who makes the last move loses the game.
